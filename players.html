Top Players 2015

Key Players of the 2015 Season

Lionel Messi

Messi was instrumental in the 2015 season, leading the team with 58 goals and numerous assists across all competitions. His performance in the Champions League final was crucial for Barcelona's success.

Neymar Jr.

Neymar scored 39 goals during the season, playing a key role in Barcelona's attacking trio. His contributions in the Copa del Rey and Champions League were vital to securing the treble.

Luis Suárez

Suárez provided 25 goals and 20 assists, forming a lethal attacking trio with Messi and Neymar. His goal in the Champions League final sealed the victory for Barcelona.
